Interfaces


PCIe Gen4 x4

One x4 lanes at a x4 slot (slot named as PCIE0-3)

Two x4 lanes at a x8 slot (slot named as PCIE8-15)

An additional PCIe adapter card will be required for fully utilize these two x4 lanes

M.2 M-KEY

One PCIe Gen4 x4 lanes at M.2 M-KEY connector for NVMe SSD, up to 22110

USB4 at Type C Connector

Two USB4 interface at Type C connectors.

The feature of each USB4 interface as below

  • USB3.x 10Gbps data transfer speed
  • DisplayPort Alt Mode
  • 5V/2A power delivery

COM Port

COM1_SER0 and COM2_SER1 are two individual headers. 2.0mm pitch

Both of them are sourced from COM-HPC module.

Pin Signal Pin Signal
1 N.C 2 N.C
3 UART_RX 4 UART_RTS
5 UART_TX 6 UART_CTS
7 N.C 8 N.C
9 GND 10 N.C

COM3 and COM4 are two individual headers. 2.0mm pitch

Both of them are sourced from a Super I/O (eSPI_CS1 enabled required)

Pin Signal Pin Signal
1 DCD 2 DSR
3 RX 4 RTS
5 TX 6 CTS
7 DTR 8 RI
9 GND 10 N.C

Jumpers for COM3 (named as MODE0/MODE1) for RS232/RS485/RS422 selection

Mode MODE0 MODE1
RS232 (default) 2-3 1-2
RS485 1-2 2-3
RS422 1-2 1-2

COM3 and COM4 are from Super I/O. Super I/O communicates with CPU through eSPI bus and Super I/O locate at carrier board. eSPI_CS1 need be enabled for the usage and it can be set-up at BIOS menu. eSPI_CS1 default is disabled

CPU FAN

4-pin connector. The FAN control signals sourced from COM-HPC module

Pin Signal
1 GND
2 +12V_5V_FAN
3 SENSE_FAN
4 PWMOUT_FAN

System FAN

Two system FAN connectors. All are 4-pin.

The FAN control signals sourced from Super I/O (need enable ESPI_CS1 at BIOS)

Pin Signal
1 GND
2 +12V_5V_FAN
3 SENSE_FAN
4 PWMOUT_FAN

Power Input

24-pin and 4-pin power connector. All of them must be connected

CPU (COM-HPC module) is powered by the 12V from 24-pin and 4-pin

Once the mechanical switch of power supply switch-on, the system will power on directly
