How to use Dediprog to flash BMC Firmware

1. Get BMC chip first you'd like to flash 2. Please put BMC chip into the socket of your Dediprog and connect to your host machine ![image-20220127111817257](HowToUseDediprogFlashBMC.assets/image-20220127111817257.png) 4. Run "Dedoprog App" and it will automatically detect the chip type 5. Click [here (version; 1.04b)](https://hq0epm0west0us0storage.blob.core.windows.net/$web/public/COMe/Ampere/AVA/Firmware/BMC/rom_v1.04.0b.zip) to download and extract it. Add the **.ima** file we provided ![image-20211122130245101](HowToUseDediprogFlashBMC.assets/image-20211122130245101.png) 6. click the "Batch button" to flash until the process is completed. ![image-20211122130336515](HowToUseDediprogFlashBMC.assets/image-20211122130336515.png)
