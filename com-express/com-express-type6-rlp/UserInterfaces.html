Note : COM Express Type 6 Raptor Lake-P dev kit consists of a COM Express Type 6 module powered by 13th Gen Intel® Core™ and a COM.0 R3.1-compliant carrier board. This page aims to lay out the user’s interface of COM Express Type 6 Raptor Lake-P dev kit.
